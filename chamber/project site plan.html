Ubuntu Youth Hub – Project Site Plan

Site Name

Ubuntu Youth Hub

The name reflects unity and collaboration inspired by the African philosophy “Ubuntu,” meaning “I am because we are.” The website promotes youth empowerment, community engagement, and shared growth.

Optional Domain: ubuntuyouthhub.org

Site Purpose

The Ubuntu Youth Hub website will provide a centralized online platform where young people can find information about youth programs, community activities, leadership opportunities, and upcoming events.

  • Learn about youth initiatives
  • Stay updated with announcements and events
  • Participate in programs and volunteer opportunities
  • Contact youth leaders for additional information

Scenarios

  • What youth events or activities are happening this month?
  • How can I join or participate in youth programs?
  • Who can I contact for more information about youth activities?
  • Where can I see announcements or updates from the youth department?

Color Scheme

  • Dark Green (#1B5E20): Headings, navigation bar, footer.
  • Gold (#FFC107): Buttons, highlights, accents.
  • Light Gray (#F4F6F5): Page background.

Green represents growth and community, while gold symbolizes energy, positivity, and youth empowerment.

Typography

  • Poppins: Primary font for headings and body text.
  • Sans-serif: Fallback font if Google Fonts fails to load.

Poppins was selected for its modern appearance, readability, and mobile-friendly design.
